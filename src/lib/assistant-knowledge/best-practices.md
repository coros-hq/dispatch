# Mailshot Starter Templates

When a user describes what they need, suggest or load the closest template.
Use the `load_template` action with the exact templateId shown below.

## tpl_minimal
Name: Minimal
Simple single-column newsletter. Text heading + body + button.
Good for: content creators, personal newsletters, simple announcements.
Trigger words: minimal, simple, clean, newsletter, basic.

## tpl_product_launch
Name: Product Launch
Hero image + headline + body + CTA button.
Good for: new product, SaaS feature launch, physical product announcement.
Trigger words: product launch, new product, announcing, release, feature launch.

## tpl_weekly_digest
Name: Weekly Digest
Dark header + numbered article list with dividers.
Good for: curated content, link roundups, weekly recaps.
Trigger words: digest, weekly, roundup, articles, issue, recap.

## tpl_welcome_email
Name: Welcome Email
Warm welcome heading + body + Get Started CTA.
Good for: SaaS onboarding, new subscriber, new customer.
Trigger words: welcome, onboarding, getting started, new user, first email.

## tpl_promotional
Name: Promotional
Blue discount header (50% OFF) + body + CTA.
Good for: sales, discounts, limited-time offers.
Trigger words: promo, promotional, discount, offer, sale, coupon.

## tpl_flash_sale
Name: Flash Sale
Red urgent header (24 HOURS ONLY) + urgency copy + unsubscribe footer.
Good for: flash deals, Black Friday, time-limited sales.
Trigger words: flash sale, urgent, 24 hours, Black Friday, limited time.

## tpl_cold_outreach
Name: Cold Outreach
Plain single-column text email with personalization placeholders + book a call CTA.
Good for: B2B sales, cold email, partnership pitch.
Trigger words: outreach, cold email, sales email, pitch, B2B, prospecting.

## tpl_follow_up
Name: Follow Up
Short follow-up text email with schedule a call CTA.
Good for: sales follow-up, re-engagement after no reply.
Trigger words: follow up, follow-up, check in, no reply, second email.

## tpl_order_confirmation
Name: Order Confirmation
Green confirmed header + order details + track order CTA + footer.
Good for: ecommerce, receipts, purchase confirmations.
Trigger words: order confirmation, receipt, invoice, purchase, transaction.

## tpl_company_update
Name: Company Update
Logo header + shipped/numbers/coming sections + social + footer.
Good for: monthly company updates, product changelogs, investor updates.
Trigger words: company update, monthly update, changelog, what we shipped.

## tpl_brand_newsletter
Name: Brand Newsletter
Premium 4-canvas editorial newsletter. Dark navy (#1a2340) theme, image grids, hero, logo strip.
Good for: agencies, premium brands, high-production newsletters.
Trigger words: brand newsletter, premium, editorial, dark theme, professional, multi-canvas.

---

When suggesting a template, always explain why it's a good fit.
Example response: "The Weekly Digest template fits well here — it has a numbered article structure with dividers that works great for curated content roundups."
